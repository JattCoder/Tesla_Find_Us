This application..

1. Find Superchargers using city, state or zipcode
2. This Application will plan a route from point a to point b (Keep your car charged on your way to destination)
3. You can add your create, update or delete your own superchargers
4. Superchargers created by you, can be used by other users for their route but other users can not delete or edit your supercharger.
5. Other users can locate your supercharger using city, state or zipcode
6. This Application will plan a route based on car (Range) you purchase from Tesla Marketplace (Hint: Cars are FREE :))
